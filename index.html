The GOAT: Ogre2
Halo Professional
2003 - 2016

Tom Ryan, known to the Halo community as Ogre2, was an inspiration to younger players. Ogre2, along with his brother Ogre1, showed that discipline and passion would lead to success in any field. When most parents were hoping their kids would quit videogames, Ogre2 showed that playing games could make a living.

Tom Ryan won the most tournaments in competitive Halo history. He shared most of his winnings alongside his twin brother, Ogre1. Ogre2 won at least 1 tournament across all but one Halo game in the series. That is sheer domination!
